WHO I AM

Strategy. Story. Results.

Every product has a purpose, but not every product has a narrative. My career has been focused on a single mission: taking high-stakes, technical products and translating them into clear, high-converting human stories. My work operates at the intersection of consumer psychology, data metrics, and graphic system design.

At Devon Technologies, this focus allowed me to construct the narrative framework that scaled MEETSESSION to 500 users in a single month, and coordinate the adoption of JudicAI across 56 Nigerian courts. When translating value for corporate and physical sectors, I designed corporate brands like Stone Oak and real estate identities like Wana Homes, designing every asset to inspire trust and drive conversions.

I became the first student to present at a joint USAID conference on digital mental health campaigns because I build narrative systems that work. I don't just design portfolios or run marketing budgets; I build sustainable client acquisition engines.
